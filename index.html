This page presents project- and master thesis proposals for the Autosit project. The Autosit project (2019-2022) is a competence-building research project funded by the Research Council of Norway, with co-funding from DNV GL, Kongsberg Maritime and Maritime Robotics. The main objective of the Autosit project is to enable autonomous ships to predict the intentions of other vessels as part of their situational awareness software. It builds on the Autosea project and is closely related to the Autoferry project.
